## Overview

**Become What They Fear** is a retro-inspired GameBoy-style adventure game centered around the afterlife and psychological fear mechanics.

You play as a ghost who can possess people, discover their fears, and strategically scare them to progress through each level.

The core gameplay revolves around observation, possession, and manipulating environments to fill a **spooky meter** before confronting your main target.

## Core Mechanics

- Possession / unpossession system
- Fear-based progression mechanics
- Spooky meter system tied to gameplay events
- Target-based level objectives
- Environmental and NPC interaction

## Gameplay Rules

- While possessed, characters cannot be scared.
- Each level contains a specific target.
- The target can only be scared after filling the spooky meter.
- The spooky meter increases by scaring other characters.

## Controls

### Menu
- Navigate: Arrow Keys  
- Select: K  
- Back: M  

### In-Game
- Move: WASD  
- Possess / Unpossess: K  
- Scare / Interact: M  
- Return to Level Selector: Backspace  

## Design Highlights

- Strategy-driven scare system
